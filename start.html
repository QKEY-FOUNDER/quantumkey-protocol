What is the QuantumKey Protocol?

A unified architecture that integrates intent, cryptographic identity, semantic agents, and alignment-oriented governance into a single coherent protocol.

Who is it for?

Researchers, engineers, protocol designers, DAO creators, and curious builders who want to create digital systems aligned with human values.

How to participate

1) Read the core documents.
2) Open issues or submit PRs.
3) Contribute to discussions (GitHub Discussions).
4) Test upcoming mini-SDKs when released.
